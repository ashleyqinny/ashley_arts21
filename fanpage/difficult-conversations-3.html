3. Mind

Practicing self-care and reflecting on conversations with yourself

A study published in Ethnic and Racial Studies found that Asian Americans who encountered COVID-19-related discrimination experienced higher levels of anxiety and depression. Another review of 121 studies found that youths who experienced discrimination were more likely to develop chronic mental health problems. Taking care of your mental health is just as important as taking care of your physical health. From dealing with burnout to controlling anxiety, it is important to understand how to navigate the emotional distress you may be dealing with.

- Practice daily affirmations.
- Connect with your community.
- Create a sacred space.
- Create a gratitude list.
- Get enough sleep and exercise.
- Practice meditation.
- Get outside.
